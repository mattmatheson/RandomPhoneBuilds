Origin Story
Mortician Turned
Country Vampire

Born in Smyrna, Tennessee and now based in Cookeville, Dan Spencer is what happens when you raise a kid on gospel quartets and John Denver, let him discover Slipknot's Vol. 3 at age 11, and then put him through mortuary school.


He spent his twenties touring with a rock band before deciding music was a dead end. He enrolled in mortuary school in Nashville, studying both embalming and funeral directing while working full-time at a funeral home. The exposure to death, the distinctive regional idioms of older community members, and the raw reality of mortality became the bedrock of his songwriting.


When his solo recordings attracted label and management attention, he dropped out of mortuary school with one semester remaining. The rest is an improbable ascent — from fry cook to funeral home to opening for Post Malone at Nissan Stadium.


He calls his sound "Country Cult Baroque" — a term borrowed from the late singer-songwriter Judee Sill. It's country, alt-folk, dark comedy, and black metal aesthetics colliding in a way that shouldn't work but absolutely does.


"Everybody's serious but me."

Journey
The Ascent
1993
Born in Smyrna, TN
Raised on gospel quartets, John Denver, and The Eagles. Discovers KISS live cassette.
2004
The Awakening
Discovers Slipknot's Vol. 3 at age 11. Terrorizer Magazine opens the door to Slayer, Darkthrone, Mayhem.
2010s
Touring Years
Spends his twenties in rock bands (Holy Coast, Pumpkinseed), touring the underground circuit.
2019
Mortuary School
Enrolls in Nashville mortuary school. Works at a funeral home — embalming, funeral directing. Death reshapes his art.
2020
Solo Recordings Begin
Pandemic era. Starts writing and recording solo material. Shelves debut due to COVID.
MAR 2022
First Release
"I Like to Worship the Devil" EP drops. Two months later, debut album "Bursting With Country-Fresh Flavor."
2023
Ian Shelton Discovers Him
Militarie Gun's Ian Shelton hears "Tennessee Buddha" and "Fat Vampire." Becomes a vocal champion.
EARLY 2024
Post Malone Connection
Connects with Post Malone through a Nashville pizza restaurant. Writing sessions for F-1 Trillion with Brad Paisley, HARDY, ERNEST. Signs to Feeling Grey Records.
MAY 2024
Bonnaroo + CMA Fest
Performs "Fat Vampire" during Militarie Gun's Bonnaroo set. Plays CMA Fest.
JUN 2024
Return to Your Dark Master
Sophomore album on Feeling Grey Records. Concept album inspired by reading Dracula. Full band arrangements.
SEP-OCT 2024
F-1 Trillion Tour
Opens for Post Malone nationwide. Plays Nissan Stadium in Nashville — the biggest show of his life.
NOV 2025
Weave Your Web Liar Man
Third album. 10 tracks. The catalog deepens.
FEB-MAR 2026
22-City Headlining Tour
Houston to Nashville. His own name on the marquee. Full circle.
