Critical Issues

Critical

No portfolio or work section

A filmmaker's website without browsable work is a restaurant with no menu. Visitors see 4 GIFs and have nowhere to go deeper. No client names, no project context, no video embeds.

Critical

No video reel anywhere on the site

You're selling video production and there is zero video playback on the entire site. A showreel should be the first thing people experience — autoplay hero, one click away, something.

Critical

No social proof

No testimonials, no client logos, no "worked with" section. For a service business, trust signals are the difference between a bounce and a booking.

Critical

No about page or personal brand

Clients hire people, not services. There's no face, no story, no "why Matt" on the site. Cookeville is a small market — personality and trust matter even more.

Warning

Homepage is a dead end

Four GIF thumbnails all linking to the same /services page. No scroll depth, no narrative, no progressive disclosure. Someone lands and either clicks Book or bounces — no middle ground.

Warning

Every page has identical footer content

Same 4 GIF thumbnails repeated on every page. Feels template-y and wastes screen real estate that could showcase different work.

Minor

Dead cart page, thin SEO, heavy GIFs on mobile

Empty Squarespace commerce page is discoverable. Almost no indexable text for Google. GIF files are bandwidth-heavy on cellular.

What's Working (Keep These)

Keep

Public pricing

Bold, transparent, filters tire-kickers. Most competitors hide rates. This builds trust immediately and pre-qualifies leads. The budget dropdown on the contact form reinforces this.

Keep

Clear positioning

"Filmmaker & Photographer / Commercial, Brand, Music+" — immediately communicates what you do and who you serve. Don't dilute this.

Keep

Three service verticals

Production, Real Estate, Social — clean segmentation. Clients can self-select. The tiered social packages are well-structured.

Proposed Site Structure

/ — Hero reel + portfolio highlights + testimonials + CTA
/work — Portfolio grid, filterable (Commercial, Music, Real Estate, Social)
/work/[project] — Individual project: video embed, BTS photos, client context
/services — Three verticals with anchor links to rates
/rates — All pricing on one page, clean and scannable
/about — Bio, photo, gear, process, personality
/contact — Form + Calendly embed for direct booking
/gallery/[client] — Client delivery pages (like the Militarie Gun page)

Design Direction

◼

Dark Theme

Your work pops on black. Every top-tier filmmaker site goes dark — it's cinema, not corporate.

▶

Video-First Hero

Full-bleed autoplay muted reel. Immediate impact. This is what you sell — lead with it.

▦

Grid Portfolio

Hoverable thumbnails that play on hover. Click for project detail with full video + BTS photos.

★

Social Proof Bar

Client testimonials, logos if available, and an Instagram feed pulling from @mattmatheson.creative.

Aa

Minimal Type

Inter or similar. Huge whitespace. Let the work breathe. Swiss-grid inspired layouts.

↗

Sticky CTA

Persistent "Book Today" button in nav. Never more than one click from conversion.
