Recomp Meal Plan 🔥

7 days of mostly whole foods to build muscle and burn belly fat at the same time. Skinny-fat → lean and jacked.

🎯 The Goal: Body Recomposition

Eat at a small deficit, keep protein high, lift hard. Your body pulls energy from fat stores while it builds muscle. The scale barely moves — the mirror changes a lot. 154 lb · 5'8" · lifting 3–4×/week.

  • 🍽️4 feedings a day, ~40g protein each. That hits the muscle-building threshold every meal — one of the four is your shake.
  • 🏋️Load carbs around your lifts. More rice/potatoes on training days, fewer on rest days.
  • ⚖️Maintenance is ~2,400 cal. 2,200 is the fat-loss number — adjust ±100 after watching your 2–3 week trend.
  • 📸Track waist + photos, not the scale. Recomp keeps weight flat while fat drops and muscle grows.

👨‍🍳 Sunday Batch-Prep

Knock these out and the week runs itself ⏱️

  • Sheet-pan 2–3 lbs chicken breast 🍗 — portion into 6 oz servings
  • Boil a dozen eggs 🥚 — grab-and-go protein all week
  • Big pot of chili 🌶️ (beef + beans) — 4–5 bowls, freezes great
  • 3–4 overnight oats jars 🥣
  • Stock up on microwaveable rice + frozen veg bags 🥦
