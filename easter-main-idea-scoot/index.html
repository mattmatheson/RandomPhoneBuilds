Finding the Main Idea in Paragraphs

🥚 Scoot Cards 🥚

Read each paragraph. Choose the best main idea.

1
🐰

Rabbits are wonderful pets. They are soft and fun to hold. Rabbits can learn to come when you call their name. They need fresh hay, water, and a clean cage every day. Many families enjoy taking care of pet rabbits.

What is the main idea?

A. Rabbits eat hay every day. B. Rabbits make great pets. C. Rabbits are soft and furry.
2
🥚

Decorating Easter eggs is a fun tradition. First, you hard-boil the eggs. Then, you dip them into cups of colorful dye. You can also use stickers, glitter, or crayons to make them special. Every egg looks different and beautiful!

What is the main idea?

A. You need to boil eggs first. B. Decorating Easter eggs is a fun activity. C. Stickers make eggs look pretty.
3
🌼

Spring brings many changes outside. Flowers begin to bloom in gardens and fields. Baby birds hatch from their eggs in nests. The days get longer and warmer. People start spending more time outdoors.

What is the main idea?

A. Baby birds hatch in spring. B. Many things change when spring arrives. C. People like to go outside.
4
🐣

Baby chicks need special care when they are born. They must stay warm under a heat lamp. They drink water from a small dish. They eat tiny bits of special chick food. Without this care, baby chicks could get sick.

What is the main idea?

A. Chicks drink from a small dish. B. Heat lamps keep chicks warm. C. Baby chicks need special care to stay healthy.
5
🍫

Chocolate is one of the most popular Easter treats. Stores fill their shelves with chocolate bunnies and eggs. Some chocolates have cream or caramel inside. Kids love finding chocolate in their Easter baskets. It is a sweet part of the holiday!

What is the main idea?

A. Stores sell lots of candy. B. Chocolate is a favorite Easter treat. C. Some chocolates have caramel inside.
6
🌸

Easter egg hunts are exciting for kids. Children search in the grass, behind trees, and under bushes. Some eggs have candy inside, and some have small toys. The person who finds the most eggs sometimes wins a prize. Everyone has a great time looking!

What is the main idea?

A. Some eggs have toys inside. B. Kids hide behind bushes. C. Easter egg hunts are exciting and fun for kids.
7
🐇

Carrots are very good for you. They help your eyes stay strong and healthy. Carrots have vitamins that help your body grow. You can eat them raw, cooked, or even in soup. Rabbits are not the only ones who should eat carrots!

What is the main idea?

A. Carrots are a healthy food to eat. B. Rabbits love to eat carrots. C. You can cook carrots in soup.
8
🎨

Easter baskets come in all shapes and sizes. Some are made of wicker and some are made of plastic. People fill them with candy, eggs, and small gifts. Many children wake up on Easter morning to find a basket waiting for them. Making an Easter basket is a lovely tradition.

What is the main idea?

A. Baskets can be wicker or plastic. B. Easter baskets are a special tradition. C. Children wake up early on Easter.
9
🌷

Tulips are popular spring flowers. They come in many colors like red, yellow, pink, and purple. Tulips grow from bulbs that are planted in the fall. When spring comes, the bulbs sprout and beautiful flowers appear. Gardens look amazing with rows of colorful tulips.

What is the main idea?

A. Tulips come in many colors. B. You plant bulbs in the fall. C. Tulips are beautiful spring flowers.
10
🐥

Ducks are fun to watch at the pond. They dip their heads under the water to find food. Baby ducks follow their mother in a straight line. Ducks have special feathers that keep them dry, even when they swim all day. You can often hear them quacking from far away!

What is the main idea?

A. Ducks quack very loudly. B. Ducks are interesting animals to watch. C. Baby ducks walk in a line.
11
🌞

Playing outside in spring is the best! You can ride your bike on the sidewalk. You can fly a kite when the wind blows. Some kids play tag or jump rope with friends. Spring weather makes it perfect for outdoor fun.

What is the main idea?

A. Kids like to ride bikes. B. Spring is a great time to play outside. C. The wind blows kites in the air.
12
💐

Many families have special meals together on Easter. Some cook a big ham or roast. Others make their favorite side dishes like mac and cheese or green beans. Everyone sits around the table together. Sharing a meal on Easter is a way to show love to your family.

What is the main idea?

A. Ham is a popular Easter food. B. Families share special meals on Easter to show love. C. Mac and cheese is a good side dish.
