The Origin Story

From a Vintage Camper to Downtown

It started with a robin's egg blue camper, a dream, and a whole lot of espresso. Tyler & Anna Dunn converted a vintage trailer into a coffee shop on wheels — serving up specialty coffee at events, evenings, and weekends across Cookeville.

In 2019, they went all in. Every dollar they had went into transforming a 100-year-old photography studio on Broad Street into the downtown coffee shop Cookeville didn't know it needed — original hardwood floors, exposed brick walls, and that unmistakable navy & gold art deco warmth.

Then came Easy Tiger Coffee Roasters. Then a second location behind Tennessee Tech. The dream kept growing — because when you love coffee and love people, good things happen.
