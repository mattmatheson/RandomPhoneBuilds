Our Story

From a vintage camper
to the westside.

SoulCraft is the passion project of Tyler and Anna Dunn — a young married couple who turned a love for coffee into a dream. Tyler used to buy vintage espresso machines off eBay in high school, rebuilding them and learning the craft from the inside out.

In 2017, they bought a vintage camper, converted it into a coffee trailer with a robin's egg blue finish, and started traveling around Cookeville on evenings and weekends. The little blue trailer quickly became the talk of the town.

The demand grew until they took a leap of faith — their entire life savings — to open a brick and mortar inside a 1920s building on the historic westside. Original hardwood floors, exposed brick, navy and gold art deco. In the summer of 2019, SoulCraft Coffee was born.
