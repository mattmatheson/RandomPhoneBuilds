Shot List — Outdoor / Nature Video

Vibe: Peaceful. Serene. "Come here, relax and get away." — PJ's words verbatim. 30 seconds. Cinematic bookend structure.

Opening Sequence — Cinematic Establishing

  • 1
    Wide drone — plateau reveal
    Open on a slow drone pull-back from the Cumberland Plateau edge, revealing the scale of the landscape. Morning light. Misty gorge if possible.
  • 2
    Tree canopy / trail entrance
    Low, slow push through the beginning of a trail into the woods. Light breaking through the canopy. Sets the tone.
  • 3
    Waterfall — static wide + slow push
    Cumberland Mountain State Park or Obed River area. Long exposure-style motion blur if using ND filter. Both a wide establishing shot and a tighter closeup of water over rocks.

Nature B-Roll

  • 4
    Hiking trail — hikers in frame
    Two people walking away from camera down a trail. Natural, not posed. Use Nathan or talent. Shot from behind, sunlight in front of them.
  • 5
    Lake / water reflection
    Byrd Lake or Lake Holiday. Still water, reflection of trees. Either drone overhead or tripod at water level with a wide angle.
  • 6
    Wildlife / nature detail
    Wildflowers (April bloom), moss on rocks, birds, whatever shows up. Macro or tight shots for texture variety in the edit.
  • 7
    Plateau overlook — drone wide
    Drone shot from a plateau escarpment looking out over the valley. The scale shot. Crossville sits at ~1,900 feet — use that elevation.
  • 8
    Fairfield Glade area trails
    Wooded trail shots. Mix of tight path shots and wider shots where trees frame the background. Good backup location if state park doesn't cooperate.

Outro — Cinematic Human Moment

  • 9
    Back porch — two people, morning coffee Key shot
    PJ specifically called this one out. Two people sitting on a back porch in the morning, tipping coffee, relaxed. Feels like a retreat. Needs the right porch — trees in background, soft morning light. Find a location that reads Cumberland Plateau, not suburban.
  • 10
    Logo end card — "Play the Plateau"
    5–7 seconds. explorecrossville.com. Same as Golf V1 end card treatment.
